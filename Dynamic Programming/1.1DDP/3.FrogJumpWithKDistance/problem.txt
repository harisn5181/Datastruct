Dynamic Programming: Frog Jump with k Distances (DP 4)


In this article, we will learn about "Dynamic Programming: Frog Jump with k Distances (DP 4)"

Problem Statement:  Frog Jump with K Distance/ Learn to write 1D DP

Problem Statement:

This is a follow-up question to “Frog Jump” discussed in the previous article. In the previous question, 
the frog was allowed to jump either one or two steps at a time. In this question, the frog is allowed to jump up to ‘K’ steps at a time. If K=4,
 the frog can jump 1,2,3, or 4 steps at every index.
